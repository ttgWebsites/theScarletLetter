Interesting Details

  • In this fictional novel Hawthorne often alludes to historical figures and details that actually excised.
  • The witch in the novel that tries to corrupt Hester is named Ann Hibbins; in real life Ann Hibbins existed in Salem, MA and was executed for witchcraft.
  • Recently a Hollywood production told a loosely based, modern version of the novel. The movie is titled ‘Easy A’ and stars Emma Stone.
