Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Set in the 1600s in Boston, it tells the story of Hester Prynne who has daughter, Pearl, with a man other than her husband. Hester does not give detail on who the father is, and is forced to carry the burden of her sin everyday by wearing a scarlet colored letter A on all of her clothing. The novel follows Hester's journey to establish an identity for herself after being an outcast in her community. It also follows the storyline of her real husband, Roger Chillingworth, who comes into town after Hester's child is born and learns of Hester's sin. Roger puts his effort into troubling both Hester's life, and her lover, who is determined to be Arthur Dimmesdale, the town reverend. The Scarlet Letter tells story of three particular characters and how they deal with the same sin.
