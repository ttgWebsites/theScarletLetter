Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Scarlet Letter
  • Author
  • Nathaniel Hawthorne
  • Published
  • 1850 by Tickner, Reed & Fields.
  • Genre
  • Historical Fiction, Romance, Symbolic.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in the 1600's in Boston, Massachusetts.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is written in the third person from an omniscient narrator who knows perhaps more about the characters than they do themselves.
  • Conflict
  • Hester Prynne, a married woman, has moved to Boston, however she was not joined by her husband. Coping with loneliness in a new home, she falls in love with another man and has an affair resulting in a child. She must then face the shame of her community for the affair. The husband, who is actually the towns Reverend, must cope with not being able to show affection or be apart of the child's life because of what it would mean to his reputation.
